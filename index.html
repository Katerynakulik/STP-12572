About the Game

Look, I know what you're thinking - another match-3 game, right? But this one's actually different Hello Kitty Merch Match goes beyond traditional match-3 by adding a strong collection layer. Players don’t just complete levels — they gather items, decorate spaces, and interact with familiar characters. There's something weirdly satisfying about watching your little collection grow with each level

How to Play

Okay, so here's the deal - once you start playing, you're gonna have a hard time stopping

  • Match Items

    Combine three or more identical pieces to clear them.

  • Trigger Combos

    Create larger matches for stronger effects.

  • Complete Goals

    Finish level objectives to earn rewards.

  • Collect Merchandise

    Unlock themed items after each level.

  • Decorate Spaces

    Use collected items to customize your world.
